Summary

Inception is a science fiction film that explores the concept of dreams within dreams. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, specializes in the art of extraction—stealing secrets from deep within a person’s subconscious during the dream state. When offered a chance to have his criminal record erased, he takes on a seemingly impossible task: performing inception, planting an idea in someone’s mind.

Main Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Elliot Page as Ariadne, and Tom Hardy as Eames.
